# CrossFit Msida

F15 Training Centr runs CrossFit classes in Msida seven days a week. The gym sits a short drive from Ta' Xbiex, Gzira, Pieta and Birkirkara, with parking on site and bus routes running along the Msida seafront. If you live or work in central Malta, this is the closest coach-led CrossFit box to your door.

We opened in Msida because it connects the north and south of the island. Members commute in from Sliema before work, from Valletta at lunch, and from San Gwann and Mosta in the evening. Most people reach us in under 15 minutes.

## CrossFit classes at F15 Training Centr

Every class is coached from start to finish. You get a warm-up, a skill or strength piece, a conditioning workout, and a cool-down. Group sizes stay small so the coach can correct your squat, scale the workout to your level, and actually learn your name.

Programming covers the full CrossFit toolkit:

- Olympic lifting: snatch, clean and jerk, and their variations
- Strength: back squat, front squat, deadlift, press, bench
- Gymnastics: pull-ups, muscle-ups, handstand work, ring work
- Conditioning: rowing, assault bike, ski erg, running, wall-ball, burpees, double-unders

The programming is intermediate by default. Movements are scaled every session so a first-week member and a five-year member can train side by side in the same class.

## Class times in Msida

The Msida gym opens at 05:30 on weekdays. The first class is at 06:00. We run 12 classes a day through to 20:00, which means you can drop in before work, over lunch, after school pickup, or after a long day at the office. Weekend hours are shorter, with late-morning classes on Saturday and Sunday.

Typical weekday slots: 06:00, 07:00, 08:00, 09:30, 12:00, 16:30, 17:30, 18:30, 19:30. Check the live schedule when you book to confirm the exact times for the day you want.

## Why train CrossFit in Msida

Msida is one of the few parts of Malta where you can train early, shower, and still make it to a Valletta or Sliema desk before 09:00. The location also suits University of Malta students and staff, since the campus is a few minutes up the road in Tal-Qroqq.

Inside the gym you get:

- Full Olympic lifting platforms and competition bumpers
- Concept2 rowers, ski ergs and assault bikes
- Rigs for pull-ups, ring work and rope climbs
- Sleds, sandbags, wall-balls and dumbbells in every weight

The space is built for group classes, open gym, and HYROX prep without anyone queueing for kit.

## What a first session looks like

Your first class runs like any other class, with one difference: the coach knows you are new. You arrive ten minutes early, introduce yourself, and walk through any injuries or recent training history. During the workout the coach scales each movement so you finish strong rather than broken.

If you have never done a barbell lift or a pull-up, book the beginner on-ramp instead. It runs in short blocks and covers the squat, deadlift, press, clean, snatch and basic gymnastics before you join the main class schedule.

Expect to be sore for two or three days after your first session. That settles within two weeks as your body adapts.

## The coaches

The Msida coaching team are CrossFit Level 1 and Level 2 certified, with backgrounds in Olympic weightlifting, competitive CrossFit, rugby, and endurance sport. They coach because they train, and they train because they coach. You will see them lifting, running and sweating in the same gym you do.
